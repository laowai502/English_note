# Model's essential factors


* Neck length is genetically determined and cannot be changed.
* The length of one'neck is primarily determined by genetics and is largely unalterable.
* Neck length has mostly to do with your genes, it's pretty much set from birth.


You need to know there are two essential factors for being model: First is having a small face, and the second is a relatively long neck. Of these two, the length of your neck is more important because it is primarily determined by genetics and is largely unalterable, unlike the face, which can be modified through surgery.
Let's be honest, a small face is simply non-negotiable for a model. It's the basic entry ticket. The real differentiator, however, is neck length. While you can technically sculpt a jawline, you can't lengthen a neck. That crucial line is a gift of pure genetics.
